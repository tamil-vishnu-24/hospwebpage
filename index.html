ORTHODONTIC TREATMENT

In today’s world instant gratification is key. When it comes to seeing results, especially cosmetic, the faster the better. With most dental and orthodontic treatments instant results are not typically guaranteed. However, there are many ways to help maximize and speed up your results.

TOOTH COLOURED FILLING

This is the dental restorations designed to be inconspicuous and natural in appearance. They blend well with the teeth and appear more natural than amalgam fillings, which are darker and more easily seen by other people.

ROOT CANAL

This is a treatment to repair and save a badly damaged or infected tooth instead of removing it. This process used to be painful earlier , but with dental advances and local anesthetics, most people have little if any pain with a root canal.
